Actors Of Tollywood

Mahesh babu

Description

Mahesh Babu is a popular Telugu film actor known for his charm, versatile acting, and massive fan following. Born on August 9, 1975, as Mahesh Ghattamaneni, he is the son of veteran actor Krishna. Mahesh made his debut as a child artist and later established himself as a leading actor in Telugu cinema with his first major film Rajakumarudu (1999). Known as the “Prince of Tollywood,” Mahesh is celebrated for his performances in hits like Okkadu, Pokiri, Srimanthudu, and Bharat Ane Nenu. His style, dialogue delivery, and screen presence have garnered him numerous awards, including several Nandi and Filmfare awards.

Pavankalyan

Description

Pawan Kalyan, a prominent Telugu actor, filmmaker, and politician, is known for his charismatic on-screen presence and powerful performances in Tollywood. Born on September 2, 1971, as Konidela Kalyan Babu, he is the younger brother of renowned actor Chiranjeevi. Fondly called "Power Star" by fans, Pawan rose to fame with blockbuster films like Gabbar Singh, Attarintiki Daredi, and Tholi Prema. Known for his unique style and intense action roles, he enjoys a massive fan following. Beyond cinema, Pawan is also active in politics, founding the Jana Sena Party in 2014, aiming to serve the people and address social issues. His influence spans both entertainment and public service, making him an iconic figure in Telugu cinema and Andhra Pradesh politics.

Raviteja

Description

Ravi Teja, often hailed as the "Mass Maharaja" of Telugu cinema, is known for his energetic screen presence and distinctive acting style. Born as Ravi Shankar Raju Bhupatiraju, he made his mark with his comic timing and action-packed performances, earning a huge fanbase. His breakthrough came with Itlu Sravani Subramanyam (2001), followed by major hits like Idiot (2002), Vikramarkudu (2006), and Kick (2009). Ravi Teja is celebrated for his relatable characters, often playing the spirited, underdog hero with a mix of humor and intensity. Known for his hard work and passion for cinema, he continues to be a prominent force in Tollywood, captivating audiences with his unique charisma and versatility.

Vijaydevarakonda

Description

Vijay Deverakonda is a popular Telugu actor known for his charismatic screen presence and bold film choices. He gained immense fame with the 2017 blockbuster Arjun Reddy, which showcased his intense acting style and established him as a youth icon. Born in 1989, Vijay hails from Hyderabad, Telangana, and entered the industry with small roles before rising to prominence with movies like Pelli Choopulu and Geetha Govindam. Known for his rugged looks and unique fashion sense, he has a massive fan following, especially among young audiences. Vijay is also an entrepreneur, having launched his clothing brand, Rowdy. His unconventional roles and powerful performances have made him one of Telugu cinema's most sought-after actors.
